## The one-sentence pitch (locked v1 · for Monday discussion)

> **Trophē is an AI-assisted nutrition coaching tool for professional nutritionists managing a roster of clients.**

That's it. Every feature should justify itself against that sentence. Walk into Monday saying this sentence; leave Monday with Michael saying the same sentence the same way.

## What Trophē is

- A **tool for the coach**, not an app for the client. The nutritionist is the paying customer. The client experience exists to serve the coaching relationship.
- **Nutrition-specific**. Food logging, habit engine, macro targets, plan creation. That's the core.
- **AI-assisted, not AI-replaced**. The coach stays in the loop — AI parses food, suggests meals, flags patterns; the coach makes decisions.
- **Methodology-native**. Encodes Precision Nutrition's habit-based approach + Michael's Kavdas protocol adjustments. This is a moat vs. generic nutrition apps.
- **Mobile-first for the client, desktop-friendly for the coach** — the two experiences need different defaults.

## What Trophē is NOT

- **Not a fitness tracker**. Nikos confirmed: workout module doesn't belong. We will log basic workout context if it helps nutrition decisions (training day = more carbs), but we are not building Strava.
- **Not a DIY consumer app**. People tracking calories without a coach have MyFitnessPal / Cronometer / Yazio. We don't compete there.
- **Not a full wellness platform**. We don't do sleep, meditation, mental health, supplements-as-primary, form checking. Those are different products.
- **Not a CRM or scheduling tool**. Nutritionists already have calendars and payment systems. We integrate with them, we don't replace them.
- **Not culturally-generic**. We are Greek-first by virtue of Michael's practice; English as the fallback. We don't try to serve every cuisine equally.

## The elevator pitch variations

- **30 seconds**: "Trophē is the tool nutritionists use to run their practice. One coach, ten to fifty clients, AI-assisted food logging, habit-based coaching methodology. Monthly subscription per coach; clients free."
- **To a coach**: "You know how you manage clients in WhatsApp and spreadsheets? Trophē is the purpose-built version of that, with AI doing the busywork (food parsing, pattern detection) so you spend time on the coaching, not the admin."
- **To a client**: "Your coach uses Trophē to track your progress. You log food by text or photo; AI figures out the macros; your coach sees patterns and adjusts. You don't need to think about numbers — that's your coach's job."
- **To an investor** (when relevant): "SaaS for a B2B niche (nutritionists) with strong retention (clients stay 6-18 months), defensible via methodology encoding, expandable to gyms/clinics (multi-coach tenants)."
